Handles Adaptive Streams — Not Just Simple Files

Many platforms use HLS or DASH streams with segmented files. UniversalDL parses the manifest, downloads segments in parallel, and stitches them with FFmpeg without quality loss.

Platforms like YouTube, Twitch, Vimeo, and Bilibili rely on adaptive streaming. UniversalDL is built to handle those formats reliably.
